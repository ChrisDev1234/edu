Lawyer vs Attorney Comparison

Aspect Lawyer Attorney
Definition A person who has studied law and holds a law degree. A lawyer who is licensed to practice law and represent clients.
Education Must complete law school (e.g., Juris Doctor or equivalent). Must complete law school and pass the bar exam.
License May or may not have passed the bar exam or be licensed. Must pass the bar exam and be licensed in at least one jurisdiction.
Practice Scope Can provide legal advice in non-licensed capacities (e.g., consulting, academics). Can represent clients in court and perform all legal duties.
Court Representation Cannot represent clients in court unless licensed. Legally authorized to represent clients in court.
Common Titles Legal consultant, law professor, paralegal (if unlicensed). Attorney-at-law, counsel, advocate, solicitor (in some countries).
Usage A general term for someone with legal training. A specific term for a licensed legal practitioner.
